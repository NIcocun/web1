What I’m Focused On Now

A snapshot of what life looks like at the moment, written on March 15, 2026.

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Location

Currently traveling full-time and recently spending time in Bali.

Current Season

I’ve been in Bali for three months now, and it feels so nice to relax after constantly moving around. I’ve been going to the gym again, taking a yoga class or two every day, and trying to finish online high school. I’ll probably finish in one to two months. I recently took a 10-hour handpan training and have been practicing a lot.

I’m assisting a Yoga Nidra training with Emily Kuser later this month, and in July I’ll be assisting a 200-hour Power Yoga Training with Paul Teodo. I’ve put my YouTube channel on the back burner for now. I still love making videos, but instead of putting pressure on myself to make the best videos possible, which was making me hate making videos, I’ve decided I’m just going to upload whenever my heart calls me to.

Same thing with my newsletter. I stopped sending it weekly because I wasn’t liking the quality of the stuff I was sending out. I prefer to put out less content and make it as good as possible. I’m not sending weekly newsletters anymore, but maybe some months I’ll send one newsletter and some months I’ll send 10. I prefer it this way because I’m not tied to a schedule.

I’m also currently working on a web app for another project. So many opportunities have been appearing out of nowhere for me recently, and I’m so grateful. I was telling my parents the other day that I feel like I’m living my own version of The Surrender Experiment. It’s an autobiography by Michael A. Singer about his decision to stop letting personal fears, desires, and ego-driven preferences dictate his life. Instead, he chose to surrender to the unfolding flow of life and accept whatever presented itself, which led to extraordinary personal success, profound inner peace, and a billion-dollar company.

I don’t have a billion-dollar company yet, but all these small things appearing right now seem like they don’t make sense at all in terms of how they’re connected. I trust that in the future, the dots will somehow connect.
