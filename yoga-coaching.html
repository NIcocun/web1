How I Teach

I care more about honesty, consistency, and having fun than performance.

My approach is grounded, encouraging, and playful. The Yoga Sutras of Patanjali tell us to remain “stable” and to be “comfortable.” I’m not trying to force you into perfect-looking shapes, but rather ones where you can breathe deeply and keep your heart open. I’m trying to help you feel better in your body, understand what you’re doing, and create something real enough to stay with you beyond a single class.

  • Support for complete beginners or people returning after time away.
  • Sessions shaped around your body rather than a generic sequence.
  • Space for both physical practice and the deeper inner side of yoga.
